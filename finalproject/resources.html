üè† Renting Tips & Rights

What to Look For:

  • Check for proper heating and cooling systems
  • Test all appliances and electrical outlets
  • Inspect for water damage or pest issues
  • Verify parking availability and restrictions
  • Understand utility responsibilities
  • Review lease terms carefully

Your Rights as a Tenant:

  • Right to a habitable living space
  • Protection from illegal rent increases
  • Right to privacy (24-hour notice for entry)
  • Right to return of security deposit
  • Protection from discrimination

Frequently Asked Questions

Student housing typically ranges from $200-500 per person for shared housing, with private rooms ranging from $400-800 per month. Many students share apartments or houses to reduce costs.

Most students prefer to live within 2 miles of campus. There's a free campus shuttle service, and many areas are bike-friendly. Consider your transportation preferences and class schedule.

Rexburg winters can be harsh with snow and temperatures below freezing. Look for properties with good heating systems, insulation, and snow removal services. Budget extra for heating costs.

Pet policies vary by property. Many rentals near campus have no-pet policies, while family housing areas are more pet-friendly. Always ask about pet deposits and monthly pet fees.
