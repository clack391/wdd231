Getting Started Guides

First-Time Home Buying

1. Check Your Credit

Review your credit score and history. Aim for 620+ for conventional loans.

2. Save for Down Payment

Plan for 3-20% down payment plus closing costs (2-5% of home price).

3. Get Pre-approved

Obtain pre-approval letter from a lender to show sellers you're serious.

4. Find a Realtor

Choose an experienced local agent who knows the Rexburg market.

5. Start Shopping

Use our property search to find homes within your budget and preferences.

Renting Tips

  • Read the lease agreement carefully before signing
  • Document existing damage with photos
  • Understand your rights as a tenant in Idaho
  • Budget for security deposit (usually 1-2 months rent)
  • Check if utilities are included in rent
  • Ask about pet policies if you have pets
  • Verify parking arrangements and guest policies
  • Keep copies of all rental documents
