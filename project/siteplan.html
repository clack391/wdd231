Rexburg Home Finder

Site Plan

Site Name

Rexburg Home Finder

This name was selected because it clearly communicates the site's purpose and geographic focus. "Rexburg" immediately identifies the local market, while "Home Finder" indicates the primary service of helping people locate housing. The name is straightforward, memorable, and directly addresses the needs of students, families, and professionals looking for housing in the Rexburg area.

Optional domain: rexburghomefinder.com

Site Purpose

The Rexburg Home Finder serves as a comprehensive local housing platform that simplifies the process of finding rental and purchase properties in the Rexburg area. The site provides property search functionality with detailed listings, neighborhood information, local amenities data, and essential resources for both first-time buyers and renters. By focusing specifically on the Rexburg market, the site offers more targeted local information than national real estate platforms, including proximity to BYU-I campus, local transportation options, and community-specific resources that matter to residents and students in the area.

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

"I'm a new BYU-I student looking for an apartment within walking distance of campus for under $400 per month. What rental options are available, and how do I contact the property owners?"

Scenario 2:

"My family is relocating to Rexburg and we want to buy a 3-bedroom house. What neighborhoods are safest for children, and what are the average utility costs in different areas?"

Scenario 3:

"I'm graduating soon and need to understand the home buying process in Rexburg. What local real estate agents and mortgage lenders should I consider, and what's the typical down payment requirement?"

Color Schema

Primary Blue (#2E86AB)

Used for: Headers, navigation links, primary buttons, and key call-to-action elements

Secondary Green (#A23B72)

Used for: Accent elements, property price highlights, contact buttons, and success messages

Neutral Gray (#F18F01)

Used for: Background sections, borders, secondary text, and subtle design elements

Dark Gray (#333333)

Used for: Body text, main content, and general typography

Typography

Primary Font: 'Roboto' (Headings)

Used for all headings (h1-h6), navigation menu items, and prominent labels. This sans-serif font provides excellent readability and a modern, professional appearance.

Secondary Font: 'Open Sans' (Body Text)

Used for body text, descriptions, form labels, and general content. This font offers excellent readability across all devices and complements Roboto well.

Accent Font: 'Montserrat' (Special Elements)

Used sparingly for property prices, special callouts, and branding elements to add visual interest and emphasis.

Wireframes

Mobile View

Mobile design prioritizes vertical scrolling with collapsible search filters and simplified navigation.

Desktop View

Desktop layout utilizes horizontal space with sidebar filters, grid-based property display, and integrated map view.
