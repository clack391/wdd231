Cost of Living Information

Utilities

  • Electricity: $80-120/month
  • Gas: $40-80/month
  • Water/Sewer: $30-50/month
  • Internet: $30-60/month

Transportation

  • Gas: $3.20-3.60/gallon
  • Car Insurance: $100-150/month
  • Parking: Often free in residential areas
  • BYU-I Shuttle: Free for students

Groceries

  • Monthly groceries: $200-400/person
  • Dining out: $8-15/meal
  • Fast food: $5-10/meal

  • Student housing: $300-600/month
